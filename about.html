Our Company

Finger Lakes Timber Company, Inc. is a leading provider of sustainable forestry and quality timber products. With a long history in the industry, we have built a reputation for responsible land management, environmental stewardship, and customer satisfaction. We specialize in providing timber solutions that balance economic, social, and environmental considerations.

Our team of experienced foresters and timber professionals is dedicated to delivering high-quality products and services while protecting the health and diversity of our forests. We utilize state-of-the-art equipment and techniques to ensure that every project is completed to the highest standards of sustainability. Whether you need timber harvesting, forest management planning, or land clearing services, we have the expertise and resources to get the job done right.

Our Commitment

At Finger Lakes Timber Company, Inc., we are committed to practicing sustainable forestry and providing our clients with the best possible service and quality. We believe in building long-term relationships based on trust, reliability, and mutual respect. Our goal is to exceed your expectations and become your trusted partner for all your forestry and timber needs.
